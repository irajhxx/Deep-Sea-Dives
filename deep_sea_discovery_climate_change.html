Reduce Plastic Waste

Prevents ocean pollution and protects marine creatures from ingesting or getting entangled in plastic debris.

Support Sustainable Fishing

Ensures fish populations remain healthy and ecosystems balanced.

Protect Coral Reefs

Safeguarding reefs helps preserve biodiversity and supports coastal protection.

Reduce Carbon Emissions

Combat climate change to prevent ocean warming and acidification, which harm marine ecosystems.

Marine Protected Areas (MPAs)

Creating no-fishing zones allows ecosystems to recover and thrive.

Clean Up Beaches And Oceans

Regularly removing trash reduces pollution and protects marine wildlife from harm.
