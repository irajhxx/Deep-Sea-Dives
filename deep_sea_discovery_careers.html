Careers!



Marine careers are vital for our planet in terms of ocean ecosystems protection, sustainable fisheries, and combating climate change. Your studies and conservation work protect the biodiversity of marine life and keep our oceans healthy. By pursuing a marine career, you can directly contribute to saving our planet. You will play a very important role in protecting ocean ecosystems, preserving marine species, and fighting climate change.Your efforts will go a long way toward creating a healthier and more sustainable world for the coming generations. Now, let's dive in and make a difference!





Marine Biologist:


Marine Biologists study marine organisms, their behaviors, and interactions with the environment. This includes researching ecosystems, species, and the impact of human activities on marine life

Skills

Demand Increase From 2022-2032

3%

Regular Tasks Include:

  1. ● Conducting surveys of marine species in a particular area to monitor and map populations and their movements

  2. ● Collecting samples (of seawater, animal or plant tissue, sand, etc.) to take back to the lab for analysis, e.g. testing the pH level of the water or checking for pollutants

  3. ● Assessing the environmental impact of proposed new construction projects or developments

  4. ● Writing and publishing reports of your findings and presenting them to governments or at conferences
